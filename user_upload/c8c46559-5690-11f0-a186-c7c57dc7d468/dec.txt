The Taj Mahal is a magnificent white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India, commissioned by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and widely regarded as one of the world's most beautiful buildings, a masterpiece of Mughal architecture. Construction began in 1632 and was largely completed by 1648, with further additions and finishing touches extending to 1653.
